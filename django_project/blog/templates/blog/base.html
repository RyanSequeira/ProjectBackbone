About Us

Project Backbone is committed to informing you about all the wonderful small businesses in your area because they are the backbone the economy! It has always been important to support your local businesses, and it is especially so during the COVID-19 pandemic. We're here to help you give back to your community so we can all be safer, happier, and more connected.
